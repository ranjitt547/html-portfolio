The best movies according to Ranjit

My top 3 movies of All-time.

Interstellar

A team of astronauts embarks on a daring journey through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet, facing existential challenges and exploring the boundaries of love, time, and space.

Avatar

On the lush alien world of Pandora, a paraplegic ex-Marine becomes part of a conflict between the indigenous Na'vi and human colonizers, discovering the beauty and peril of a new life in an extraordinary ecosystem.

3 Idiots

Three engineering students navigate the pressures of academic life, friendship, and societal expectations in a humorous and heartfelt journey that challenges the conventional education system.
